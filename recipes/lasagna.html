Lasagna

Description

This is a simple lasagna recipe that is quick and makes a really tasty lasagna.

You can share this dish with the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Vegan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the tofu sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
